About Us

What You Need to Know About Us

At Keedo Preschool, we believe in providing a happy, secure and enriched learning environment for children aged 3-5 (Nursery & Reception). We believe that by ensuring our children feel valued and safe, and by offering an array of opportunities to build their self confidence and develop key learners skills, we can pave the way for them to flourish throughout both our Primary and Secondary schools.
